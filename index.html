Welcome to For the Youth

The For the Youth is a Non-Profit Organization that was founded by Asemahle Khumalo after she saw children of schooling age, playing on the streets on a school day. She did some research and found that these children are not financially capable to pay school fees and essentials. For the Youth aims to bridge that gap by providing school necessities such as school uniforms, stationery and toiletries.
