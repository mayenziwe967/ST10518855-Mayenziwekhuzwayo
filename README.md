WEBSITE CHOICE 

 

Name: For The Youth 

Organization Description:  

Education is not only essential but also a right for all citizens of South Africa. It provides skills and knowledge- things like reading, problem-solving, writing, and how the overall world works. These skills are essential for everyday living and securing a job. Education opens loads more opportunities. This includes a broader range of careers, higher earning potential, and flexibility in what one wants to do.  

Many children in Eshowe (Kwa-Zulu Natal) spend their school days playing on the streets. This was observed by Asemahle Khumalo, who then further researched the situation out of worry and curiosity. She found that most of these children come from households that lack the financial capability to afford schooling essentials such as school uniforms, stationery, and toiletries. Since then, Asemahle has founded an NPO called “For the Youth” which provides the mentioned schooling essentials to children in primary and high school. This helps children to gain the skills and knowledge at school that they need to navigate the world.  

“For the Youth” aims to provide the youth with proper schooling essentials and to ultimately have every child attend school with dignity regardless of their financial situation. Not only does school provide the education needed, but it also gives room for growth and improving social awareness. They learn about different cultures, religions and perspectives which makes them better at working with others.  

Reasons for choosing the organization: 

“For the Youth” is an organization that prioritizes the education of the youth. This prepares them for future employment and independence, allowing them to support themselves. This helps break the prevalent cycle of poverty, providing the opportunity to uplift their families, which benefits communities over time. It encourages innovation and progress, creating new technologies and ideas while building responsible citizens that value responsibility and respect helping them contribute positively to the community.  

The importance of education and the organization’s values and goals lead to the choosing of this website. This is an organization that strives to give the youth of South Africa a better future through a dignified schooling experience. This will, ultimately, better the community and the rest of society.  

 

 

WEBSITE GOALS AND OBJECTIVES 

 

The website wishes to solve the problem of the lack of awareness and visibility of the organization. With a website, more people will become aware of the organization’s existence. People all over the world will learn about the cause and vision. The website will stand as a hub of information for the website.  

The website wishes to draw more volunteers, sponsors, and donors. As the organization gains more visibility and awareness from the community, this will attract possible volunteers, sponsors, and donors. This will benefit the organization as more schoolchildren will be able to receive the essential schooling supplies. This will lead to reaching the organization’s goal of having children attend school regardless of thier financial situation.

DESIGN AND USER EXPERIENCE 

 

Design aesthetic and branding 

 

The website boasts a warm and welcoming atmosphere, making it attractive and increasing traffic to the website. A warm atmosphere invites people and increases interaction with the website.  

Design style: vibrant with a modern creative lifestyle 

Inspiration: bold sunset-inspired colour gradients 

 

Colour Scheme 

 

Deep red: primary brand colour, header and footer gradients 

Vibrant purple: secondary gradient colour, navigation highlights 

Beige: main page background 

White: content containers and text contrast 

Dark grey: body text and supporting content 

Pink accent: section headings and decorative borders 

 

Font 

 

Outfit (Sans Serif): used throughout the website. (headings, content and footer info) 

 

Typography 

 

	Modern and clean appearances with excellent readability across all devices. 

 

Layout and design approach 

 

	Full-width gradient background for the header section. Rounded navigation buttons with 	smooth hover transitions for the navigation. Rounded corners plus soft shadow effects for 	depth in the main content area. Balanced spacing between elements and large imagery in 	the content structure.  

 

User experience 

	Simple menu structure with easy access to all major sections. Mobile-friendly navigation 	layout. Lightweight design structure with efficient CSS transitions. There is high colour 	contrast between the text and the backgrounds. This provides a raedable and consistent 	visual hierarchy.  

 

Visual design features 

	White background with rounded corners (15px). Soft shadow effects and clean separation 	from page background. Hover effects of navigation colour transitions, image scaling 	animations and image shadows.
