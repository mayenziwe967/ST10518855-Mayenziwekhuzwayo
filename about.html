About Us

The organization was founded by Asemahle Khumalo in March 2022 after seeing many kids playing on the streets on a school day. She did some research and found out that these kids come from families that lacked the financial capability to afford schooling essentials.This sparked something in her and so she created the For the Youth Organization. This organization is built on the values of equity and access, whereby every child deserves school necessaties regardless of their financial situation. Dignity and respect, whereby support is given to uplift the learners without shame. education empowerment whereby, education is the way to change and opportunity.

Mission

To provide the youth with proper schooling essentials

Vision

To have every child attend school with dignity regardless of their financial circumstances
