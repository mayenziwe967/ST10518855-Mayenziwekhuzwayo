Volunteer with us

Volunteers provide unbelievable support and change to the childrens lives and are always needed. Without volunteers, this organization would not function. They provide the manual support and are the driving force of For the Youth.

Benefits of volunteering

  • It provides a sense of purpose and self-esteem with a natural sense of accomplishment.
  • It enhances your physical health.
  • It improves mental health as it counteracts anxiety and depression providing a sense of community.
  • It helps you develop new skills as it provides training and hands-on experience.
  • It provides you with the opportuinity to network with people from various professional background that could lead to mentorship or job opportubities.

Volunteer responsibilites

  • Program delivery: this includes distribution of school necessities and toiletries.
  • Community engagement: this includes running awareness campaigns and coordinating with sponsors.
  • Fundraising: this includes hosting fundraising events and managing donations.
  • Administration: this includes maintaining the records of beneficiaries and inventory.
  • Logistics: this includes managing storage and sourcing uniforms, stationery and toiletries.

Volunteers are encouraged to participate as many hours as they possibly can in a week.
